Admission Information

Welcome to the AHIA Admission Portal for the 2025-26 academic session. We offer a range of programs to empower students with quality education. Apply now to secure your seat!

Eligibility Criteria

  • For Undergraduate Programs: Minimum 50% marks in 10+2 from a recognized board.
  • For Postgraduate Programs: Minimum 55% marks in a relevant Bachelor's degree.
  • Entrance Exam: Candidates must qualify AHIA Entrance Test (AHIAET) or equivalent (e.g., CUET).
  • Age Limit: Below 25 years as of July 1, 2025.

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: June 1, 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: July 15, 2025 (02:15 PM IST)
  • Entrance Exam Date: August 10, 2025
  • Result Declaration: August 25, 2025
  • Counseling: September 1-5, 2025

Application Process

  1. Register online at the AHIA official website.
  2. Fill out the application form with personal and academic details.
  3. Upload scanned copies of required documents (e.g., mark sheets, ID proof).
  4. Pay the application fee of INR 1,000 via online payment.
  5. Submit the form and download the confirmation for future reference.
