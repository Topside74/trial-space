About Our Academy

Al Hidaya Islamic Academy (AHIA) is an esteemed Islamic educational institution situated in Kalamassery, Kochi, Kerala. Affiliated with Darul Huda Islamic University run Njalakam Muslim Jama'ath, Chemmad, AHIA is dedicated to providing a comprehensive blend of traditional Islamic scholarship and contemporary education, aiming to nurture morally upright and intellectually capable individuals.
