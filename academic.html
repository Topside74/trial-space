Academic Overview

At Al Hidaya Islamic Academy, we are committed to providing a holistic education that integrates traditional Islamic sciences with modern academic disciplines. Our academic framework is designed to foster intellectual growth, moral values, and professional skills.

Academic Programs

Courses Offered

  • Hudawi - Advanced Islamic Studies and Theology
  • Zahrawiya - Arabic Language and Literature
  • Hifz - Quranic Memorization and Recitation
  • Mahdiya - Islamic Jurisprudence and Fiqh

These programs cater to students from various educational backgrounds, offering both undergraduate and postgraduate levels with a focus on Islamic scholarship and contemporary education.

Faculty

  • Dr. Ahmad Khan - Head of Islamic Studies (Hudawi)
  • Prof. Fatima Zahra - Arabic Literature (Zahrawiya)
  • Shaykh Ismail - Quranic Sciences (Hifz)
  • Dr. Yusuf Ali - Jurisprudence (Mahdiya)

Our faculty comprises experienced scholars and educators dedicated to mentoring students in their academic and spiritual journeys.

Research

Al Hidaya Islamic Academy encourages research in Islamic studies, comparative religion, and educational methodologies. Students and faculty collaborate on projects to contribute to global Islamic scholarship.

  • Ongoing Research: Islamic Education in the Digital Age
  • Publications: Annual AHIA Research Journal

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art Library with Islamic and Modern Texts
  • Digital Learning Labs
  • Hostel Accommodation for Students
  • Prayer Halls and Meditation Rooms
