The College

AL HIDAYA islamic academy (AHIA)

Al Hidaya Islamic Academy (AHIA) is an esteemed Islamic educational institution situated in Kalamassery, Kochi, Kerala. Affiliated with Darul Huda Islamic University Run by Njalakam Muslim Jama'ath, Chemmad, AHIA is dedicated to providing a comprehensive blend of traditional Islamic scholarship and contemporary education, aiming to nurture morally upright and intellectually capable individuals.

Principal's Desk

Abdul Salam Quasimi


I praise my Lord for blessing me with the opportunity to serve at Al-Hidaya.
I take pride in the managing committee of Njalakam Jama'ath for affiliating this institution with Darul Huda and operating it in a context that is relevant to the times.
The right path is the goal of both Darul Huda and Al-Hidaya. It is the right path that Allah has revealed in the Qur'an (Al-Baqara).
If, because of you, a single person is guided to the right path, it is better for you than the whole world and everything in it. (Hadith)
There is no doubt that this institution is creating milestones of transformation within the Muslim community.
I hope that the leaders of tomorrow will be the children of this institution. With the humble request that your prayers and support be with all the endeavors the institution puts forward…

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

  • To develop an Islamic educational framework that nurtures individuals conscious of their ultimate responsibilities—towards Allah.
  • Themselves, their families, and society—by practicing Islam and promoting religious scholarship.
  • Guided by the Qur’an, Prophetic legacy, and Islamic heritage, unifying knowledge with truth and purpose.

Vision

  • Become a center of excellence in Islamic education, reviving scholarly mastery in Islamic sciences.
  • Establish research hubs, preserve intellectual heritage, and raise a generation of true scholars (Ulama).
