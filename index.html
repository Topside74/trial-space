Al-Hidaya Islamic Academy is an institution focused on Islamic and modern education, operating under the influence of Sunni scholarly traditions. Welcome to Al Hidaya


I thank Allah for the opportunity to serve at Al-Hidaya and appreciate the Njalakam Jama'ath for affiliating it with Darul Huda, shaping it to meet the needs of our time. Al-Hidaya and Darul Huda share the mission of guiding towards the right path revealed in the Qur'an, and as the Prophet said, leading even one person to it is better than the world and all it contains. May this institution continue to transform the community and raise the leaders of tomorrow—with your prayers and support.

Journey


through the Al Hidaya

The atmosphere of Al Hidaya is exceptional.  By getting admission, you enter into a global cohort who stands by the welfare of Ummah. A community of learners from different parts of India transcends the boundaries of language and strives collectively for spiritual, cultural and educational excellence. Al Hidaya is built  upon three foundational values; Ta’leem, Tarbiyah and Da’wa.
